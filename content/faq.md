# FAQ

**Is there a file size limit?**
Free accounts can upload files up to 2 GB. Paid plans raise this to 25 GB.

**Who can see my files?**
Only people with the share link. Links are unguessable and can be revoked at any time.

**Can I password-protect a link?**
Yes — toggle **Require password** when you create the share link.
