# Getting Started

SwiftDrop lets you share files with a single secure link. Upload a file, copy the
generated link, and send it to anyone — no account required on their end.

## Upload your first file

1. Sign in and open the **Dashboard**.
2. Drag a file onto the upload area, or click **Choose file**.
3. When the upload completes, click **Copy link**.

## Link expiry

Every share link expires after 7 days by default. Paid plans can extend this up to
90 days or disable expiry entirely.
